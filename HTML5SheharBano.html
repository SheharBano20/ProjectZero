Watch as lightening strike town's electric

A lightning strike temporarily knocked out power in a neighborhood near Queanbeyan in New South Wales, Australia, on February 4. You can see in the video various lights on at the bottom of the screen, then after multiple lighting flashes, everything goes dark. The power was off temporarily and has since been restored.

Six people rescued from frozen lake

On February 2nd 6 people were rescued from Lake Wallenpaupack in Pennsylvania after their snowmobiles and ATVs went through the ice. With warmer temperatures comin it's important to remember ice safety.
